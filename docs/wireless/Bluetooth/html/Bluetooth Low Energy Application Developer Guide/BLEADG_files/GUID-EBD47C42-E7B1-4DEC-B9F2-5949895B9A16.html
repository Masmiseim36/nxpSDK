Common files for hybrid applications

All the hybrid applications should use the existing files from examples/hybrid/common without editing them. These perform common initializations for both Bluetooth Low Energy and 802.15.4.

The Bluetooth Low Energy initializations are similar to the ones described in Bluetooth Low Energy initialization.

In the ApplMain.c, both the Bluetooth Low Energy and 802.15.4 functionalities are enabled in the main_task function:

/* Bluetooth Low Energy Host Stack Init */
Ble_Initialize(App_GenericCallback); 
 
/* 802.15.4 PHY and MAC initialization */ 
Phy_Init();
MAC_Init();
 
App_Init();
